Aims

The aim of this game is to find the tallest bar in a hidden graph.
Each time you click on a section of the hidden graph,this section will be revealed in the form of a black vertical bar.
The taller this black bar is, the more points it is worth.
After a bar is clicked, a window at the top of the screen will also show the selected bar, with a red line equal to the graident (the slope) of the graph at that point.
There will be several rounds, each with the same objective, but a different hidden graph to find the tallest bar of.

Instructions

You select a section of the graph by clicking on it. The first time you click, it will be outlined in red.
Click again to confirm your selection and a bar will be revealed in this section of the graph. The taller the bar the more points it is worth!
Your score will be updated if you find a bar higher than any other you have seen so far.
Highlighting a bar you have already selected will show its gradient, selecting it again will use up a trial and you wont earn any extra points.
You can end the round once you think you have the highest score, by clicking the button above the graph.

You will have up to 25 chances per round to select bars to try and find the highest.
For every one of the 25 selections you have remaining at the end of the round, you will earn a bonus point!
Eg. Ending the round after selecting 10 different bars, with 15 selections remaining, will grant you 15 bonus points.
